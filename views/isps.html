Welcome to the TT ISP Performance Dashboard

 

 

Objective: To dynamically rank the performance of local ISPs by comparing an estimate of rate achieved for a monthly cost of $200

Achieved Rate: The download rate achieved (in Mbps) using Speedtest.net with a target server in Miami at around 8pm weekdays (averaged over 3 tests)

Cost Definition: The monthly cost of your Internet data plan (VAT inclusive) not including TV/Phone.

Metric: Estimated achievable rate (in Mbps) for a monthly cost of $200TT

Trust Model: Only "trusted" users can contribute data or recommend others. A user becomes trusted when recommended by at least one trusted user.

Data Contribution: Send email to isp-perf@lab.tt with subject "ISP performance". First line of body is either Blink, Flow, Digicel, Massy or Greendot. Second line of body is achieved Rate. Third line of body is monthly Cost. Only one contribution is requested but you can re-submit at anytime and overwrite your previous value.

Recommendation Process: Send email to isp-perf@lab.tt with subject "Nominations". Body contains a list of email addresses of nominees

Dashboard Access: Presently anyone can view the dashboard (may change in the future)

Become Trusted: If you would like to contribute but are not yet trusted please send a request to admin@lab.tt
