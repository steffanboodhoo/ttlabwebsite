Welcome to the TT ISP Performance Dashboard

 

 

Objective: To dynamically rank the performance of local ISPs in terms of achieved rate per dollar spent

Rate Definition: The download rate achieved (in kbps) using Speedtest.net with a target server in Miami at around 8pm weekdays

Cost Definition: The monthly cost of your Internet data plan

Metric: Ratio of rate to cost in units of kbps/$ 

Trust Model: Only "trusted" users can contribute data. A user becomes trusted when recommended by at least one trusted user.

Data Contribution: Send email to isp-perf@lab.tt with subject "ISP performance". First line of body is either Blink, Flow, Digicel, Massy or Greendot. Second line of body is computed metric value.

Recommendation Process: Send email to isp-perf@lab.tt with subject "Nominations". Body contains a list of email addresses of nominees

Dashboard Access: Presently anyone can view the dashboard (may change in the future)
